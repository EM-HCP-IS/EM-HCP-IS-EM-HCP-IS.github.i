The Workshop on Engineering Methodologies for Human-Cyber-Physical Intelligent Software

January 21-22, 2025 | Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore

Welcome

We are pleased to invite you to the Workshop on Engineering Methodologies for Human-Cyber-Physical (HCP) Intelligent Software. This workshop will bring together leading experts from around the world to discuss the latest advancements in engineering intelligent software in the context of human-machine-object integration.

Key topics include automated requirements engineering, architecture design, coding, testing, software security, and formal verification for HCP intelligent software.

Key Highlights

  • Expert Lectures & Presentations: Renowned scholars will share cutting-edge research across various domains, including requirements engineering, coding, testing, and architecture design.
  • Forums & Panel Discussions: In-depth discussions on key challenges and future directions of intelligent software engineering in the era of Human-Cyber-Physical integration.
  • Networking & Collaboration Opportunities: Engage with top researchers and academics from NTU, NUS, SMU, and other prestigious institutions (e.g., Peking University and Nanjing University) to foster collaboration across disciplines.
